Ingredients:
 -1 pack of maggi
 -2 cups water
 -salt to taste
 -chopped vegetables
 -coriander leaves

 Directions:
 1.Boil water in a sauce pan and boil it.
 2.Add 1 pack of maggi to it.
 3.Add maggi masala and chopped vegetables.
 4.Add salt to taste.
 5.Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
